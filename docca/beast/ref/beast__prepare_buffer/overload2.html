prepare_buffer (2 of 2 overloads)

Get a trimmed mutable buffer.

Synopsis 
boost::asio::mutable_buffer
prepare_buffer(
    std::size_t n,
    boost::asio::mutable_buffer buffer);
Description

The new buffer starts at the beginning of the passed buffer. Ownership of the underlying memory is not transferred.
